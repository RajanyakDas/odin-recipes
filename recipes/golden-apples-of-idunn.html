Golden Apples of Idunn

Description

Golden Apples of Idunn are a legendary delicacy in Norse mythology, said to grant temporaryimmortality to those who consume them. A crisp, honey-glazed dessert said to grant the Aesir eternal youth.

Ingredients

  • Golden Apples of Idunn
  • 1/2 cup Wildflower Honey
  • 1 tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup Crushed Walnuts

Instructions

  1. Core the apples, leaving the bottom intact to create a "cup."
  2. Mix the honey, cinnamon, and walnuts in a small bowl.
  3. Spoon the mixture into the center of each apple.
  4. Bake at 190°C for 25 minutes until the skins are soft and the honey is bubbling.
  5. Let cool slightly and apply gold leaf for a legendary finish.
