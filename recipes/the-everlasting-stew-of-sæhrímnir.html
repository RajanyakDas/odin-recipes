The Everlasting Stew of Sæhrímnir

Description

A robust, slow-cooked root vegetable and ale stew designed to satisfy a battalion of Einherjar after a long day of combat practice.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs Thick-cut Pork Shoulder (cubed into warrior-sized chunks)
  • 4 Large Carrots (sliced into battle-ready rounds)
  • 3 Large Potatoes (cubed into shield-worthy pieces)
  • 1 Large Onion (diced into strategic pieces)
  • 4 Cloves Garlic (minced into tactical bits)
  • 2 Cups Beef Broth (the lifeblood of the stew)
  • 1 Cup Dark Ale (the elixir that gives the stew its legendary flavor)
  • 2 Tbsp Tomato Paste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, brown the pork shoulder chunks over medium-high heat until they develop a battle-scarred crust. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté the onions and garlic until they become translucent and fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the carrots and potatoes to the pot, stirring to combine with the onions and garlic. Cook for another 5 minutes to allow the vegetables to soften and absorb the flavors.
  4. Return the browned pork shoulder to the pot, then pour in the beef broth and dark ale. Stir in the tomato paste until it is fully incorporated.
  5. Bring the stew to a boil, then reduce heat to low and let it simmer for at least 2 hours, or until the meat is tender and the flavors have melded together into a legendary stew.
  6. Serve hot with crusty bread or over a bed of barley for a hearty meal fit for an Einherjar.
