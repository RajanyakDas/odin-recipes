Bifrost Berry Tart

Description

A vibrant tart inspired by the colors of the Bifrost, the rainbow bridge in Norse mythology. This tart combines a buttery crust with a medley of fresh berries, creating a visually stunning and delicious dessert that captures the essence of the mythical bridge.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted Butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/4 cup Granulated Sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a food processor, combine the flour, butter, and sugar until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Press the dough into a tart pan and bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown. Let it cool.
  4. Fill the cooled crust with a mixture of fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries) and a glaze made from honey and lemon juice.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator for at least an hour before serving to allow the flavors to meld together.
